Change Lives

By donating a skateboard or skateboard accessories, you can help make a difference in a child's life. If you cannot donate a skatebaord, a monetary donation or volunteering is another way to help the foundation. You can help change a child's life by donating to our foundation.

Why it Matters

Monetary donations are more than welcome. We use all donations to help provide skateboards and other accessories for the children. We use donations to help fund our grants that we give back to the community. All donations are used to help make an impact on each child's life by providing them with necessary supplies for skateboarding and school.
