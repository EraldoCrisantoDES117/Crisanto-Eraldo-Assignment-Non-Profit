Why Volunteer?

By volunteering you can help change someone's life, meet new people, make new friends, and build a stronger community. Volunteering helps build character and it teaches you more about autism and skateboarding. Volunteering at a local event only requires a few hours of your time, but it makes a significant impact on a child's life.
